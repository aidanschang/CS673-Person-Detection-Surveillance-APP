About FaFi

The FaFi application implements the YOLOv5 pre-trained model to detect any person objects in a live streaming video through the computer's camera. A Surveillance mode can be turned on and stores a snapshot of the video along with other information whenever people are detected inside the video, then an email notification will be sent to the email on file.
